About

What is FSA?

The purpose of the Filipino Student Association at Georgia Tech is to promote Filipino Culture and the education of Filipino traditions among students. The club intends to build relationships between students through social events, meetings, and service activities. We aspire to organize, provide, and promote active participation in civic, social, and charitable activities within the Georgia Tech, Asian American, and Filipino community. We also desire to create and foster a positive, friendly, healthy, and safe social atmosphere for those interested in Filipino Culture, and to bring together students of diverse backgrounds and cultures. We want to be known as a club that welcomes students and helps them learn about Filipino culture by sharing Filipino food with them during events, cultural dance workshops, karaoke nights, and game nights that have activities that will relate to Filipino culture.

Media Noche



New Years traditions turned into games

Media Noche is a Filipino tradition inherited from the Spanish in which family and friends gather for a midnight feast on New Year's Eve as a symbol of prosperity and abundant year ahead. We had lots of good food with great people and celebrated more fun Filipino New Year's Eve traditions like jumping at midnight and filling containers with water!
